Jose Fernandez (Nino)

The Life Of One Of The Greatest Pitchers in the MLB:

  • 1992- Born in Santa Clara, Cuba
  • 2005 - Ramon Jimenez, Fernandez's stepfather, defected from Cuba and settling in Tampa, Florida. On three occasions, Jose unsuccessfully attempted to defect, each failed defection attempt was followed by a prison term.
  • 2007 - On Jose's fourth attempt to defect, at the age of 15, he successfully defected with his mother and sister. During the period at which they were defecting Cuba, Jose's mother fell overboard when the boat hit turbulent waters and he dove in to save her life.
  • 2008 - They reached Mexico and then moved to Tampa.
  • 2009 - Ramon had Jose train with Chinea, who had trained some of Cuba's top pitchers before also defecting to Tampa.
  • 2010 - Jose began attending Braulio Alonso High School in Tampa Florida and played on the high school baseball team and became apart of the Florida Class 6A state champions in his sophomore and senior seasons.
  • 2011 - Before his senior year, the Florida High School Athletic Association ruled that Fernandez was ineligible to move to the senior grade, but MLB's Cincinnati Reds were prepared to sign Fernandez as an international free agent to a $1.3 million signing bonus and was then declared elible for his senior year. Within this year he was also drafted by the Florida Marlins.
  • 2012 - Jose began pitching for the Greensboro Grasshoppers of the Class A South Atlantic League (SAL).
  • 2013 - He moved back to the Miami Marlins and was given the 'Rookie of the Year Award', as well as the National League Rookie of the Year Award.
  • 2015 - Fernandez spent most of his season recovering from his Tommy John surgery, but in between these periods at which he was injured he recorded six strikeouts on his first day back and hit a homerun.
  • 2016 - Jose appeared in the MLB All-Star Game and had an overall 38-17 win-loss record for a .691 winning percentage and a 2.58 ERA.
  • 2017 - Five days prior to Fernandez's death, he announced that his girlfirend Maria Arias was pregnant with their child and was born in February 2017, unfortunately he passed away on a boating trip on the 25th of September and never got the chance to see her.
